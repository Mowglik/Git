Guild equipements rules

  • NEVER pull items from someone who is online. Send them a private message first & wait for the positive answer.

  • Only pull from the person’s backpack, & from the Guild Store; only a Leader can pull equipped gear.

  • NEVER go offline with Crystaline gear equipped ! Crystaline are very special pieces that have mostly been donated/bought. These pieces are very fragile and if you are caught using them WITHOUT UNBREAKABLE 190 or better, the gear will immediately be removed from your character and you will receive a warning for the first offense. The second offense and you will lose the privilege of recall items for 3 days. The third offense and you will lose the privilege of recall items for 1 weeks. Those items can be use for arena, since they don`t lose durability.

  • NEVER go offline with Fully Forge gear equipped. You will have your gear removed from your character by a Leader.

  • NEVER go offline with Epic gear equipped. Those items are usefull for everybody to hunt, GVG, Arena, etc. They are not there to boost your staminas gain. You will have your gear removed from your character by a Leader.

  • NEVER go offline with Guild gear equipped. You will have your gear removed from your character by a Leader.

  • ALWAYS return guild`s items to the player you have recalled them, FULL REPAIR !!!

  • Do not tag unless asked to hunt for it by a Leader. If you want to donate the set or pieces ask committee first.

  • Legendary gear DONATED to the guild will be tagged. These too will be for the use of all in the guild and kept in GS or unequipped when not in use.

  • Super Elite sets DONATED to the guild will be tagged. These too will be for the use of all in the guild and kept in GS or unequipped when not in use.

  • Items that have been fully forged and found to be very useful may be tagged after asking the committee Arena stuffs are very welcome too.

Buffs rules

  • Buff yourself first before you ask for a buff.

  • Do not ask for buffs you don`t need.

  • If you haven`t received the asked for buffs, go ahead and start hunting without it, or try finding someone selling them.

  • No Mass Messages or Private messages for buffs, except if people told you that you can private message them.

  • No more than 3 buffs requested, at the discretion of the buffer, not mandatory. It means you can ask for more than 3, but you may only receive 3.

  • No complaining if you do not receive all the buffs you asked for.

  • Always say thank you as your guild mate has used his/her stamina just to help you.

  • We recommand that you get to 4000 stamina fast to maximize your buffs, except maybe for GVGers

  • No specification on the level of the buff. It`s only extra if you get a high level.

  • Offer your own buffs as well, people who never buff others can be excluded from being buffed themselves. Write your buffs in your BIO.

Mercenaries rules

It isn’t as important as it once was about maintaining gold in the guild to hire mercs. However, do be aware that it takes gold to hire mercs and it is not there for just 1 or 2 people to use. It would be wise to have more people hunting when the hiring of mercs is involved. I do realize there are times during the day when it is difficult to find enough people online in the guild to get a decent group together. If it becomes a problem that 1 or 2 people are hiring mercs just for themselves we will discuss the problem with that person at that time. Just be aware that mercs can benefit a large amount of people. We need to have more merc days. I will leave the coordinating merc days to the Internal Affairs Committee for consideration for the future.

Penalties:

  • First offense- 1 day in detention

  • Second offense- 1 week in detention

  • Third offense possibility of being kicked from the guild. You will need to get in touch with one the guild founder
